AI Literacy & Prompt Engineering

Developed by and for the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam

Welcome to our educational platform dedicated to enhancing AI literacy and prompt engineering skills. This resource is designed to help educators, students, and researchers at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam understand and effectively utilize AI technologies in academic and professional contexts.

Our mission is to empower the VU Amsterdam community with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence, promoting responsible and effective use of these powerful tools.

Discover how to effectively leverage AI in your academic work and research while developing critical skills in prompt engineering.

About This Initiative

This platform is a collaborative effort by faculty, researchers, and students at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam to promote AI literacy across disciplines. We believe that understanding AI technologies and developing effective prompt engineering skills are essential competencies in today's academic and professional environments.

All materials on this site are developed specifically for the VU Amsterdam community, taking into account our unique educational philosophy and research focus.

We welcome feedback and contributions from all members of the VU community to help improve and expand these resources.
